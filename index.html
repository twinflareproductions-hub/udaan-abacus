Why Choose Us

What is Abacus?

Abacus is a powerful brain development program that helps children improve calculation skills while strengthening concentration, memory, logical thinking, and confidence. As children progress, they learn to perform calculations mentally by visualizing the abacus.

How Does It Work?

Children begin with a 1:4 Soroban Abacus and gradually develop the ability to solve calculations mentally through regular practice. This improves speed, accuracy, focus, and problem-solving skills.

Why Does It Help Brain Development?

Abacus combines logical thinking with mental visualization, encouraging both sides of the brain to work together. The result is sharper thinking, better concentration, stronger memory, and greater confidence in learning.
